Booking Guide

Welcome to OOP16! Follow these steps to book your movie tickets online easily.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Visit the Website: Go to our homepage at www.oop16.com.
  2. Select a Movie: Browse the "Now Showing" or "Coming Soon" sections to find the movie you want to watch.
  3. Choose a Showtime: Click on the movie poster to view available showtimes.
  4. Select Seats: Choose your preferred seats from the seating chart.
  5. Make Payment: Proceed to the payment page to complete your booking using various payment options.
  6. Receive Confirmation: You will receive a confirmation email with your ticket details.

Need Help?

If you encounter any issues during the booking process, please contact our support team at support@oop16.com.
